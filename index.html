Aamlat Recipe

    Here are making an aamlat recipe

    Ingredients

  • Eggs 02 Nos.
  • Oil 100 grm
  • Onion 200 grm
  • Tomato 200 grm
  • Green Chilli
  • Dhaniya need basis
  • Salt on need basis

    Method

    The Following may be applied to make aamlat

  1. First of all Tomato, Onion and Green chilli may be cut in small pieces
  2. Put the oil in Karahi
  3. Break the Eggs and mix it in to the past of tomato, onion and chilli
  4. Then put it on Karahi
  5. Keep it on Karahi for 3,4 minutes
  6. Yes, My aamlat recipe is ready to eat
